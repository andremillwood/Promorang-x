# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 18, 2026**

Promorang ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application and services.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, username, and profile information.
- **Content & Activity:** Posts, comments, likes, and interactions on the platform.
- **Economic Data:** Points, gems, and transaction history related to the platform economy.
- **Device Information:** IP address, device type, and operating system.
- **Media:** Photos and videos you choose to upload (requires camera/photo library permission).

## 2. How We Use Information
- To provide and maintain our services.
- To manage your account and transactions.
- To facilitate the "Bounty Hunt" and reward systems.
- To communicate with you about updates and opportunities.
- To ensure the security and integrity of our platform.

## 3. Sharing of Information
- With other users (public profile information and posts).
- With advertisers/merchants when you participate in their "Drops" or use their coupons.
- To comply with legal obligations.

## 4. Your Rights
- You can update your profile information at any time within the app settings.
- You can request account deletion by contacting support.

## 5. Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@promorang.app.
